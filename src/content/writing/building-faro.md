This is the first entry. I'll use this space to tell the story of building the
things in my portfolio — **warren**, **gollum**, and whatever comes next.

## Why build from scratch?

I was told a popular blog engine was the best place to start. But I didn't just
want a blog — I wanted a *home*: a place that sells what I do as a Data
Scientist, links to the tools I'm building, and keeps my CV and writing in one
fast, owned, no-database site.

So `faro` — Spanish for *lighthouse* — was born: a beacon pointing to
everything I make.
